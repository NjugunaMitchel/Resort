Our Facilities

Supirior Suite

Our Superior Suites offer spacious living with the sleeping area featuring a king size bed. The suite features a flat screen TV and mini fridge. All hotel guest rooms feature personal refrigerators, in-room valuables safes, wireless internet access, single-serve coffee maker, hair dryer and full-sized iron and ironing board. Accommodates up to two guests.
